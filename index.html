About the RescueBand

RescueBand by WildWatch is waterproof band with a GPS personal locator beacon paired with an informational app that helps people stay safe in marine environments by providing them a durable and reliable alert system with the power to call for help anywhere and at any time.

Lifesaving, Faster

Marine-based search and rescue operations rely on accurate information and specific procedures. In quickly changing conditions these efforts are easily impeded.

People need a device that helps them alert emergency responders and transmit their location immediately and reliably when they need help.
